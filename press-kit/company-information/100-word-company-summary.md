# Red Eagle Tech - Company information

## 100 word company summary

Founded in 2024, Red Eagle Tech is a UK-based software engineering company helping small and medium-sized businesses access enterprise-quality technology without the enterprise price tag. 
The company specialises in bespoke software development, building custom web and mobile applications, system integrations, data platforms, and intelligent automation tailored to each client’s needs. 
Alongside development, Red Eagle Tech provides managed IT operations and strategic technology governance, acting as a one-stop tech department for growing businesses. With a partnership-focused approach 
and fixed-price agile delivery, Red Eagle Tech serves clients across the UK and internationally.
